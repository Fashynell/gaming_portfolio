Title i "Title" is the title of the specified game.

If there are multiple games in a series that I have similar opinions about or I can't distinguish them much, they are listed in the format of: Series title (games in the series I have played).

Note that not all games I have ever played are in this list. A lot of Browser and Mobile games are too insignificant to even include, and some of them I may not have remembered or been able to find again.
Playtime i "Playtime" is the estimated amount of time I have spent playing the game.

Some of these numbers are inflated due to being AFK, and others are inaccurate due to not being able to track their playtime.
Completion i "Completion" is what state the game is in for me, or how much of it I have completed.

100% - I have done everything in the game, meaning either all achievements or what I deem to be everything.

99% - I have done almost everything in the game, except for something specified in the notes.

Finished - I have completed the game, but not much more.

Unfinished - I have not beaten the game, but I intend to do so.

Dropped - I have not beaten the game, and I don't intend on finishing it.

N/A - The game does not particularly have an end, or cannot really be "completed".
Platform i "Platform" is the gaming platform/console that I have played the game on.

If multiple, they are in order of how much I have played the game on.
DLC i "DLC" says if I have played the games' DLC or not.

Yes - I have played all DLC.

Other - I have played the specified DLC, but not all.

No - I have not played any DLC.

N/A - The game does not have any DLC (cosmetics or additional items don't count as DLC).
Rating i "Rating" is my personal opinion of how much I enjoy the game, ranked from 0 to 10.
Notes i "Notes" contains a short explanation, some opinions, or additional information about the game.
